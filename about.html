Who We Are

We’re a team of marketers, entrepreneurs, and problem-solvers who have seen the frustrations in influencer marketing—from both sides. We understand how hard it is for brands to find the right partners, and for influencers to find aligned opportunities. We created SociallyCurrent to solve that.

Why We Built This

The influencer marketing space is growing fast—expected to surpass $24 billion by 2025. But there’s still no reliable way to predict success before investing. Brands hesitate. Influencers get overlooked. Everyone loses.

SociallyCurrent was built to bridge that gap with AI, make better matches, and bring clarity and control to every collaboration.

Our Purpose

To empower creators and brands to build meaningful, data-backed partnerships that drive real results—without the guesswork.

Mission

To revolutionize influencer marketing by delivering AI-powered tools that streamline the discovery, communication, and campaign performance process—giving power back to the people behind the content.

Vision

To become the most trusted platform for brands, creators, and athletes to connect, collaborate, and scale—authentically and effectively.
